Key Factors to Consider Before Buying

Budget Planning

Include purchase price, insurance, registration, fuel cost, and yearly maintenance.

Fuel Efficiency

A fuel-efficient vehicle reduces long-term running costs significantly.

Usage Purpose

Choose based on whether you need city rides, family trips, off-road travel, or long-distance comfort.

Maintenance & Service

Select brands with strong service networks and affordable spare parts.

Safety Features

ABS, airbags, traction control, and braking quality are essential.

Resale Value

Vehicles with strong brand trust usually offer better resale returns.
