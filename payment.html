Payment & Refund Policy

This policy outlines the terms related to payments and refunds for services provided by Automotive Enthusiast.

Payment Terms

All consultation services require advance payment through our supported payment methods. Services will be provided only after successful payment confirmation.

Refund Policy

Once a consultation service has been delivered, payments are non-refundable. This is due to the time and effort involved in providing personalized recommendations.

Cancellation

If you cancel your request before the service begins, you may be eligible for a partial refund at our discretion.

Exceptions

Refunds may be considered in exceptional cases such as duplicate payments or technical errors.
