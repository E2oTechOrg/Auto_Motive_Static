Cancellation Policy

This Cancellation Policy outlines the terms for cancelling services offered by Automotive Enthusiast.

Cancellation Before Service

You may cancel your request before the consultation service begins. In such cases, you may be eligible for a partial refund as per our policy.

Cancellation After Service Starts

Once the consultation process has started or been delivered, cancellation is not allowed and no refund will be provided.

Late Cancellation

Requests made after service initiation or completion will not be eligible for cancellation or refund.
